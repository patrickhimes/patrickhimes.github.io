What interests me most about game development is the chance to create interactive experiences through game mechanics. I look forward to the challenge of boiling a game idea down to its most basic elements and then turning those elements into playable mechanics that are simple, yet build upon each other to create a complex system.

I thrive in small companies where individuals are required to perform multiple skill sets and enjoy taking on new challenges. I also love the closeness of such companies, because I find that it promotes teamwork, communication, and camaraderie.

In my free time, I continue to develop my skills by working on technical challenges and sharing that knowledge in the form of tutorials on my YouTube channel.
