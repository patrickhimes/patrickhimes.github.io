I'm currently the Director of Software Engineering at my company. While it's nice to be able to control the overall direction of our product, I really enjoy coding most of all. I look forward to the challenge of boiling a concept down to its most basic elements and then turning those elements into simple mechanics that build upon each other in order to create a complex system.

I thrive in small teams where individuals are required to perform multiple skill sets and enjoy taking on new challenges. I also love the closeness of such teams, because I find that it promotes sharing ideas, communication, and camaraderie.

In my free time, I continue to develop my skills by working on technical challenges and sometimes sharing that knowledge in the form of tutorials on my YouTube channel.
